Our Coffee Menu

Espresso

$3.00

Espresso is the ultimate concentrated coffee—a small, potent shot that packs pure energy and bold, intense flavor into every sip. It’s made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee under high pressure, which extracts a rich, full-bodied flavor and creates that signature layer of crema on top. This makes it the perfect way to kickstart your day, whether you enjoy it on its own or as the base for your favorite coffee drink like a latte or cappuccino.
