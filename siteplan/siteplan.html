Gabbi's Coffee Place - Website Plan

Site Name

Coffee To GO

This name combines a personal touch ("Gabbi's") with clear indication of the business type. It creates a friendly, approachable brand identity while emphasizing the Kampala location.

Site Purpose

  • Showcase menu items and seasonal specials
    • https://arach-code.github.io/wdd231/chamber/discover/
  • Provide location details and operating hours
  • Offer online ordering and table reservation system
  • Highlight coffee sourcing and brewing philosophy

Scenarios

  1. "What coffee blends do they offer for espresso drinks?"
  2. "How do I get to the café from the Kampala city center?"
  3. "Can I see photos of the café interior before visiting?"
  4. "Do they offer dairy-free milk alternatives?"

Color Schema

Main Brown
Cream
Accent Green
  • Main Brown (#6F4E37): Headers, accents, buttons
  • Cream (#F5E6D3): Background, card surfaces
  • Accent Green (#8AAE5C): CTAs, highlights

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display (Serif) - For elegant coffee-themed titles

Body Text: Lato (Sans-serif) - Clean and readable for content

Wireframe

Desktop View

  • Top navigation bar with logo
  • Hero image with tagline and CTA button
  • 3-column grid: Menu, Location, Events
  • Footer with social media links

Mobile View

  • Stacked header with hamburger menu
  • Vertical scroll layout
  • Full-width image carousel
  • Collapsible menu sections
